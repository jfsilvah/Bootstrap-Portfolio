About Me

I studied for 4 years in "Escuela Superior de Computo (ESCOM)" and graduated as "Systems Engineering", my first school project to have the degree was a 3D simulator to create 3D scenarios that can be used by games or prototypes for any building developed in C++.

After getting the degree I worked for 2 years developing automation systems in C++ that communicated with electrical and industrial components through serial port to manage and schedule production processes that will produce animals food or in some cases food suplements.

Next 15 years to the actual date I've worked developing and customizing Oracle ERPs or developing standalone components for customer with Oracle Databases, I have many experience working with PL/SQL, tunning queries, designing entity-relation modules for custom systems. I worked with many customers in Mexico, South America and United States.

Today I'm starting to develop sites using the languages HTML5, CSS3, Javascript and working with the Bootstrap Framework and MySQL and MongoDB databases.

I love coding and for this reason I'm giving a change on my professional career trying to learn more things and combining all my experience to offer the best solution to the customers as I've done in the last 17 years.

My hobbies are playing chess online or looking for sites to practice it, watch horror movies, playing games in my PS4 or XBOX, I prefer the shooting games or strategy games.
